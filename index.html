Welcome to Scrooge

This is a simple invoice and tracking web application suited for small firms. What can you do with scrooge?

  • Tracking your team daily job activities
  • Managing the job orders and checking their status
  • Creating an invoice
  • Generate a pdf-invoice
  • Tacking expenses
  • Tracking invoice payment status
  • View the current status (economics, in/out, activities, ...)
  • Creating users and managing them
  • Managing customers and all the lookup data

It's built on Rails 3.2.
